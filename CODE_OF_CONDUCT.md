# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

Participants are expected to communicate clearly and respectfully, focus on the work, and make space for practical technical disagreement.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, threats, discriminatory language, sustained disruption, and publishing private information without consent.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or restrict participation when behavior is inconsistent with this code of conduct.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns privately to the maintainers. Include relevant links, context, and any prior attempts to resolve the issue.
